# KVS.services — recipe (visual)

Budapest design+dev studio. A dark, sound-on WebGL experience with a deliberate "metal / underground"
edge wrapped around premium craft. Award badges (FWA Site of the Day, Awwwards) sit pinned to the frame.

## Palette (approx — from screenshots)
- **Ground:** near-black `~#0a0a0a` / `~#111`.
- **Text:** off-white `~#f4f4f2` (intro is light-on-dark inverted to dark-on-pale-fabric).
- **Accent:** ONE hot orange `~#ef6a1f` — used surgically for highlight blocks, the ACTIVE marquee tag, `CONTACT`, `SOUND ON`, and the ASCII/character-grid blocks. Everything else is monochrome.
- Single-accent discipline: orange is the only chroma; it carries all emphasis.

## Type
- **UI labels = monospace, wrapped in `{curly braces}`** (`{PREMIUM DESIGN…}`, `{BUDAPEST/}`, `{FIGMA}`, `{SOUND}`) — bracketed mono is the signature voice.
- **Logo = blackletter / black-metal gothic lettering** ("KVS") — a high-contrast device against the clean mono.
- Large geometric **"A" triangle** mark in the hero. Uppercase throughout.

## Grid & composition
- Full-bleed dark canvas with a **curved-screen (CRT) vignette** + woven-fabric/loom texture overlay across everything.
- **Corner-anchored chrome:** logo top-left, contact top-right, sound toggle, a vertical **"W. Site of the Day"** badge pinned to the right edge, FWA ribbon bottom-left.
- **Horizontal scrolling marquee** of tool tags with index numbers (`005`), the active item boxed in orange.
- Intro gate: centered **dithered halftone** monochrome illustration + an orange character-grid block, `CLICK TO ENTER · {HEADPHONES RECOMMENDED}`.

## Motion (inferred)
- WebGL/canvas; **sound-led** (headphones recommended). CRT curvature + fabric-texture shader over the whole frame.
- Dithered/halftone illustration animates; marquee auto-scrolls; intro→enter transition.

## Mood
Underground-premium: dark, textural, a little brutal, very crafted. Orange-on-black + blackletter-vs-mono is the whole personality.

## When to use
Edgy premium studio/agency or portfolio that wants attitude over polish-for-polish; "boutique with teeth." Reach for: single hot accent on near-black, bracketed-mono UI voice, a texture/CRT overlay, and one loud contrast device (blackletter logo).
